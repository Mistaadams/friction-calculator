Understanding the Visualization

Blue (Low Pressure): Near the toe of the wellbore
Yellow/Orange (Medium): Mid-lateral section
Red (High Pressure): Near surface and heel

The color gradient shows cumulative pressure buildup from friction as fluid travels through the wellbore.
